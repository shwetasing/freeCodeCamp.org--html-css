Resume

Worked on the project ‘Performance Analysis of Optical WDM Network’ under the supervision of Prof. Ajeet Srivastava, dept. of Electronics and Communication, UIETCSJMU. Working on the project - - Analysed different parameters of Optical WDM Network using MATLAB. - Analysed the Quality Factor of WDM network with respect to channel spacing. - And also found the shortest path to reduce blocking probability and implemented the algorithms in C++. ‣ Undergone six weeks training from Jun-17 to Jul-17 at Indian Railways and analysed the working of various equipments used in Signal and Telecommunications Department of Indian Railways. Gained first-hand experience of the control communication, signal processing and various public amenities in Indian Railways. ‣ Completed five weeks training on Advanced Embedded System and its Applications in Automation, under the supervision of Rajiv Shankar Sinha(Head, BRiCS) at BRiCSKanpur (Dec-15 to Jan-16). ‣ Given a seminar on the topic 5G Technology and submitted its report at UIET, Kanpur-2016.
